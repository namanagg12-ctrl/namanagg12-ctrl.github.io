About Me

A brief introduction to who I am and what I do.

Hi, I’m Naman!

I’m a curious problem-solver who loves turning raw data into meaningful stories. With a Master’s in Business and Management and a background in Electronics Engineering, I bridge the gap between technical detail and business strategy.

Over the past few years, I’ve worked on projects that involve building insightful dashboards, analyzing complex datasets, and helping decision-makers see the bigger picture. Tools like Power BI, SQL, Python, and Excel are my everyday companions, but what excites me most is using them to create real-world impact.

Beyond the numbers, I enjoy meeting new people, learning from different perspectives, and exploring creative ideas. When I’m not analyzing data, you’ll probably find me at the gym, experimenting with new recipes, or discovering hidden spots around the UK.
